How to make a mage off line:-

Set ch level to 4 = mage 5 = archmage
also need to set ch trust to same as ch level in order for mage bit to
take affect :P

6  Arch Mage
5  Mage
4  Apprentice
 
Set first field in runes to 0 - 4 depening on magic color
